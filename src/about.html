Ultra-Thin Bonded Wearing Course

Let’s Talk

Ultra-Thin Bonded Wearing Course

is used to both seal and level roadways which exhibit moderate to heavy cracking and are also in need of re-profiling. Candidates for this process must be structurally sound; any areas exhibiting base failure should first be repaired.

The process consists of an ultra-thin application of a gap-graded Hot Mix wearing course placed over a heavy application of polymer modified emulsion by a specialized machine in a single pass.

This process is suited for all types of roads, from low volume housing developments to use on the interstate highway system. The material can be applied to asphalt or concrete surfaces and will outperform all other types of Hot Mix wearing courses.

The product is excellent for use on high traffic areas due to its ability to be quickly re-opened to traffic within minutes. This system has a proven life of 10 – 15 years, under all types of conditions, making this material a highly effective and important maintenance process.

Benefits of Ultra-Thin
Bonded Wearing Course

Sealing qualities of a Chip Seal with advantages of a thin Hot Mix overlay in one process

Quick, clean application with very little disruption to residents or the travelling public

Saves on curb reveal, laid at an average depth of ¾ inch

Proven service life of 10–15 years

Superior sealing, waterproofing and leveling of the pavement

Open texture provides for future maintenance to be an inexpensive Grip Tight Fog Seal
